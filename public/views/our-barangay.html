HISTORY

The rich heritage of Barangay Baliwasan

ERA 1

BEFORE THE SPANIARDS

Four Subanon brothers lived in what is now Zamboanga City: Tubunaway, Dumulalandan, Sumolidid, and Idsak.

🏹

The original settlers of Baliwasan

ERA 2

THE SPANIARDS ARRIVED

Tubunaway, Dumulalandan, and Sumolidid moved to other parts of Mindanao, while Brother Idsak remained and resided in Tetuan.

⛵

Idsak stayed while his brothers moved on

ERA 3

ISDAK'S SON

Liwasan, and his followers settled in the area now known as Baliwasan.

📝 Did you know?

"Baliwasan" comes from the name Liwasan, the son of Idsak who first settled here.

👨‍👦

Where Baliwasan got its name

ERA 4

DESCENDANT OF ISDAK

Also his followers spread along the western coast of Zamboanga, where Subanon presence can still be noticed today.

🌊

The Subanon legacy continues

📜 The Subanon legacy lives on in the heart of Baliwasan
