Waste Segregation Guide

Biodegradable

Food scraps, yard waste, paper, wood

✓ Examples:

  • • Leftover food, fruits, vegetables
  • • Grass clippings, leaves, branches
  • • Paper towels, cardboard
  • • Eggshells, coffee grounds

Non-Biodegradable

Plastics, styrofoam, textiles, rubber

✓ Examples:

  • • Plastic bags, bottles, containers
  • • Styrofoam cups, packaging
  • • Old clothing, fabric scraps
  • • Tires, rubber materials

Recyclable

Glass, metals, paper, certain plastics

✓ Examples:

  • • Glass bottles, jars
  • • Aluminum cans, tin cans
  • • Newspapers, magazines
  • • Plastic bottles

Important Reminders

DO NOT MIX

Keep biodegradable and non-biodegradable waste in separate containers.

BE ON TIME

Place your garbage out by 6:00 AM on your scheduled day.

SECURE WASTE

Ensure garbage bags are tied securely to prevent spills and stray animals.

NO SUNDAY COLLECTION

Garbage collection is not available on Sundays.
